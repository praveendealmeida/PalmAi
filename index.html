Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our AI palm reading service.

How accurate is the AI palm reading?

Our AI has been trained on thousands of palm images and corresponding personality traits, making it highly accurate. However, it's important to remember that palm reading is an interpretive art and should be considered as guidance rather than absolute prediction.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take privacy very seriously. Your palm images are processed securely and are never stored on our servers after analysis. We don't share your personal information with any third parties.

What do I need for a good palm photo?

For best results, take a photo in good lighting with your palm facing up and fingers slightly spread. Make sure the entire palm is visible and the image is not blurry. Avoid shadows covering any part of your palm.

Can I get readings for both hands?

Yes, you can upload photos of both hands. In palmistry, the dominant hand is said to represent your present and future, while the non-dominant hand represents your past and inherent traits.
